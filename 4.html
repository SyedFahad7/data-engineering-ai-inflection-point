Industry Signals We Cannot Ignore

The Shift to Systems
2024

Restructuring as Focus

Layoffs and re-orgs were often misinterpreted as failure. In reality, they were a capital rotation: moving budget from legacy maintenance to AI readiness and data infrastructure.

Signal: Efficiency

2025

Tool Vendor Pivots

Developer tool companies (e.g., Vercel, Netlify) shifted marketing from "building blocks" to "AI-native platforms." The market stopped buying components and started buying intelligent outcomes.

Signal: Consolidation

2026

Automation & Leverage

Differentiation is no longer in writing code, but in designing systems that verify code. Value creation has moved upstream to architecture and downstream to data quality.

Signal: Leverage
