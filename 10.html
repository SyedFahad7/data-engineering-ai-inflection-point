AI Made Data More Valuable & More Difficult

The Trust Triad

Direct Monetization

In the past, data supported decisions. Now, AI systems turn data directly into product features. The data is the product.

Silent Failures

Bad code crashes. Bad data makes AI hallucinate confidently. Detection is much harder.

Harder than Modeling

Open source models are commodities. Clean, governed, proprietary data is the moat.

TRUST

Data Quality

Accuracy, freshness, and completeness. Without this, AI generates plausible nonsense.

Governance

Access control and privacy. Critical when AI can inadvertently leak sensitive data in responses.

Lineage

Traceability and reproducibility. Understanding "why" the AI gave a specific answer.
